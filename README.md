## Project Statement

This dashboard helps in understanding the type of coffee is preferred by customers. Coffee being an essential and one of the most preferred beverages consumed all over the world. In this small activity, we get to see the type of coffee alonh with its volume of sales is being visually represented.


### Steps followed 

- Step 1 : Load data into Power BI Desktop, dataset is a csv file.
- Step 2 : Clicked on "Transform data" to see if there are any errors of the CSV file that is used
- Step 3 : Also since by default, profile will be opened only for 1000 rows so you need to select "column profiling based on entire dataset" to ensure you get to see the entire data.
- Step 4 : It was observed that in none of the columns errors.
- Step 5 : A Clustered Bar Chart was used to generate the visualization of how the coffee was purchased. Light Blue indicates for card-based transactions and the Dark Blue indicates cash-based transactions. 
- Step 6 : Since the data contains the type of coffee, the times of it being purchased and the method of transaction being used to get the sale report.

           
           
           Although, by default, while calculating Sum of Coffee Sales, blank values are not ignored as they are labelled as "Cash-based transactions" With that being said there are no other empty blank values left in the data.
- Step 7 : A Pie Chart was also added to represent the percentages of the types of coffee sold using various colors for better representation. 
- Step 8 : A Gauge Chart has been added to represent the total sales by default, in case if the user wants to check a particular cofee, it will represent the total sales of that coffee sold.
